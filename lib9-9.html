The Light We Carry: Overcoming in Uncertain Times
by Michelle Obama (Author)



The Light We Carry: Overcoming in Uncertain Times is a nonfiction book written by Michelle Obama and published on November 15, 2022, by Crown Publishing. According to the Associated Press, the author "shares the contents of her 'personal toolbox' - the habits and practices, attitudes and beliefs, and even physical objects that she uses to overcome her feelings of fear, helplessness and self-doubt."[1][2][3][4] The Light We Carry: Overcoming in Uncertain Times has generally received positive reviews from book critics.
